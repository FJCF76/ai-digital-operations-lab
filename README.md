# AI Digital Operations Lab

This repository documents practical experiments exploring how AI can support the execution, operation, and scaling of digital initiatives.

The focus is not on technology for its own sake, but on understanding how AI changes the way digital businesses can be designed, built, and operated.

---

## Core Question

If AI can already support key parts of execution — including software development, infrastructure, marketing, operations, and customer interaction — how does that reshape the way we evaluate, build, and scale digital initiatives?

---

## What this repository contains

This is a collection of hands-on experiments and working artifacts, including:

- Automation workflows (e.g. n8n)
- Infrastructure setup patterns (VPS, deployment)
- AI usage patterns and prompt strategies
- Practical use cases combining AI with operational workflows

---

## Repository Structure

- **/n8n-workflows**  
  Automation workflows for operational tasks and experimentation.

- **/infrastructure**  
  Notes and patterns for deploying and managing environments.

- **/experiments**  
  Exploration of AI capabilities, prompt strategies, and learnings.

- **/use-cases**  
  End-to-end scenarios combining AI, automation, and infrastructure.

---

## Context

This lab complements **Webfiable**, a practical initiative focused on applying AI to cybersecurity and digital business.

Together, they serve as a way to explore not only what AI can do, but how it changes execution speed, operating models, and the economics of digital initiatives.

---

## Positioning

This is not a production system.

It is a working lab to explore where AI creates real leverage — and where human judgment remains critical.

---

## Approach

- Focus on execution, not theory  
- Build small, test fast  
- Capture what works and what doesn’t  
- Prioritize business impact over technical complexity  

---

## Disclaimer

All content is experimental and intended for learning purposes.
